Whispr Child Safety Standards

Whispr has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Whispr strictly prohibits any content, behavior, communication, or activity involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), grooming, solicitation, sexualization of minors, or any attempt to exploit or endanger children.

2. Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report child safety concerns or abusive behavior by contacting us at:

Email: inemerhan@gmail.com

Reports related to child safety are reviewed as quickly as possible. Accounts that violate these standards may be permanently banned, restricted, or reported to appropriate authorities where required by law.

3. Moderation and Safety Measures

Whispr takes user safety seriously. We investigate reports of harmful behavior and take action against users who violate our policies. We do not allow users to use Whispr to create, share, request, promote, or facilitate CSAE or CSAM in any form.

4. Law Enforcement Cooperation

When legally required or when necessary to protect children, Whispr may cooperate with law enforcement or relevant child safety organizations.

5. Public Availability

These standards are publicly available and apply to all Whispr users. They are referenced by Whispr and STR-C Apps for Google Play compliance.
